Authentication Types &

Who are you? How do you prove it? And who decides? — Every API must answer these three questions. This presentation covers every major approach and when to use each one.

🔐
Authentication
"Who are you?" — Verifying the identity of the caller. Is this actually Sarah, or someone pretending to be her?
🛡️
Authorization
"What can you do?" — Once we know who you are, what are you allowed to access? Different from authentication!
🏢
Identity Provider
"Who vouches for you?" — A trusted third party (Google, Azure AD, Auth0) that manages identities so you don't have to.

The Golden Rule
Authentication = proving identity. Authorization = permission to act. They are different. JWT does authentication. [Authorize(Roles="Admin")] does authorization.
